Did You Know: Direct Emails & SMS from Your AI

AI Receptionist can send emails and SMS messages directly to you or your contacts - a feature many users haven't discovered yet! Here's how to set it up:

  1. Enable "Direct emails from your AI Receptionist" and/or "Direct SMSs from your AI Receptionist" in your settings
  2. Add a custom instruction telling your AI when to send messages

For example: "If a caller asks about pricing send an SMS to David with caller details." Your AI will handle the rest, keeping your team informed in real-time.

Did You Know: Boss Mode Features

Call your AI Receptionist from your registered phone number and say "boss mode" to unlock powerful management features! Many users don't realize just how capable Boss Mode really is:

  • Review recent calls: Get verbal summaries of your latest call activity
  • Update settings on the fly: Change your AI's behavior without logging in
  • Place outbound calls: Call any number using your AI's phone number instead of your personal number
  • Call masking: Keep your personal number private by requesting "call masking" when placing calls
